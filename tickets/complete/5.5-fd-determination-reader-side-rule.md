## What landed (implement stage, commit 78f15380)

The reader rule: `isUniqueDeterminant(attrs, fds, columnCount, isSet)` — true iff
closure(attrs) covers every column AND uniqueness is reachable (the relation is a
set, or an unguarded `kind:'unique'` FD's determinants ⊆ closure(attrs)). All
FD-surface uniqueness readers (`deriveKeysFromFds`, `hasAnyKey`, `hasSingletonFd`,
`isUnique`, `keysOf`) route through it and thread `isSet`. `isSuperkey` was renamed
`closureCoversAll` (pure coverage, no uniqueness claim) to force a call-site audit;
`isAssertedKey` was deleted (subsumed — sort/window strict-monotonicOn call the
primitive directly).

With soundness moved reader-side, every producer drop gate was deleted:
`foldSingleSingleGated` (reference.ts CHECK/assertion folds, filter.ts predicate
FDs — now unconditional `addFd` as 'determination'), the `activateGuardedFds`
endpoint gate (activation is now unconditional kind-preserving `stripGuard`),
join-utils `dropSideKeyFds` + the equi-pair endpoint probe (non-preserved sides
get phase 1's `downgradeUniqueFds` only; equi-pairs emit both directions
unconditionally as 'determination'), and the project-node injective-pair gate
(matching returning-node).

Fixes two confirmed live wrong-results bugs (regression specs in
`test/fd-determination-reader-side-rule.spec.ts`): the constant-pin phantom
singleton (DISTINCT wrongly eliminated over `where a = 1 and b = 2` on a bag) and
the partial-unique guarded key FD activating above a fanning join. Restores the
direction-B payoff: kept determinations feed ORDER BY trailing-key pruning,
GROUP BY collapse (`group by a, b` → `group by a` + picker), and derived keys
above DISTINCT (the isSet branch).

Docs updated: `docs/optimizer.md` (new "The reader rule" subsection, per-operator
table rows, helper surface, join key-coverage, DISTINCT-elimination note) and
`docs/optimizer-conventions.md` (FD-aware rule conventions 1, 2, 7 rewritten
around the coverage-vs-uniqueness split).
